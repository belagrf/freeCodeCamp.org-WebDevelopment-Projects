Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is DreamTrack's sleep tracking?

DreamTrack uses advanced algorithms and your device's sensors to provide highly accurate sleep tracking, typically within a 95% accuracy range.

Do I need to wear a device while sleeping?

No, DreamTrack works with your smartphone placed near your bed. For even more accurate tracking, you can use it with compatible smartwatches.

Can DreamTrack help with sleep disorders?

While DreamTrack can provide valuable insights into your sleep patterns, it's not a medical device. Always consult with a healthcare professional for sleep disorders.
